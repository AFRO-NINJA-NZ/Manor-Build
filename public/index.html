WHY WE ARE THE FIRST CHOICE FOR COMPACT LUXURY HOMES
Why we are the first choice for compact luxury homes

Manor Build is the answer to New Zealand's Building crisis'.
Manor Homes is in the business of matching New Zealanders with their dream home with minimal hassle and maximum satisfaction.
Our homes are pre-fabricated relocatable houses that are compact, luxurious yet affordable. Every home is completed in a fraction of the time a regular build would take, and at a fraction of the price.
Manor Build offers flexible designs, ability to have as much or as little input as you wish, guaranteed delivery times, and 'mansion-level' quality.

FAST BUILD TIMES
ENJOY YOUR HOME SOONER

With more than 20 years' experience in the building industry, Manor Homes knows what works and what doesn't. Every home is constructed under close supervision at one of our yards in Auckland or New Plymouth, then relocated to its final destination. We use an intelligent systemised building process that delivers optimum efficiency and gives us the ability to build a new house in as little as 6-8 weeks, which means you spend less time worrying about the build and more time living.
